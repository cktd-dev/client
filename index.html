Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to bring?

You need to bring your original Aadhaar card, a photocopy of it, and the document for the service you want to link (like bank passbook, PAN card, etc.).

How long does the linking process take?

The actual linking process at the camp takes about 10-15 minutes per service. The confirmation from UIDAI may take 24-48 hours.

Is my Aadhaar data secure with you?

Absolutely. We follow strict data privacy protocols. We only collect the last 4 digits of your Aadhaar for identification. Your full Aadhaar details are processed securely through UIDAI's official channels.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before your slot. Cancellations with refund are allowed up to 48 hours before the appointment.
